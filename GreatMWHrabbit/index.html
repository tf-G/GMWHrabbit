Rabbits are among the Midwest's most beloved animals — playful companions, garden visitors, and fascinating creatures with a biology all their own. This guide covers the full spectrum: the Leporidae family, domestic house rabbits, wild cottontails, meat breeds, and the practicalities of rabbit ownership.

Taxonomy

Leporidae

The rabbit family — from house bunnies to snowshoe hares. Luh-POUR-uh-dee.
Pet Life

Domestic House Rabbits

Indoor-only companions with lifespans of 8+ years, coming in many wonderful breeds.
Wild

Garden Rabbits

Cottontails — a different species from domestic rabbits, living wild with a lifespan under 3 years.
Breeds

Meat Rabbits

Large, spirited breeds like New Zealand Whites — surprisingly great as adoptable pets.
