Keeping a Rabbit

Bonding Process

  1. Speed-dating at the shelter (repeat weekly as needed)
  2. Observe signs of attachment (grooming)
  3. Select partner
  4. Conduct further sessions at the shelter and, eventually, at home — increasing time together with supervision until rabbits coexist
