Companionship

Bonded Rabbits

When two rabbits form a bond, they become inseparable companions — grooming each other, sleeping together, and providing the constant company that social rabbits thrive on. A bonded pair is one of the most rewarding sights in rabbit ownership.

Mario and Luigi — brothers, bonded from birth.

Bonded rabbits are calmer, happier, and often healthier than solitary rabbits. If you're away from home for long hours, a bonded pair means your rabbit always has company. See the Bonding Process page for how to introduce a second rabbit.
