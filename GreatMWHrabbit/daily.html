Keeping a Rabbit

Daily Upkeep

  • Change litterbox 2× per week
  • Refresh hay as needed
  • Check eating: healthy rabbits munch hay around the clock
  • Feed salad 2× per day (2–3 Romaine leaves and sprigs of parsley or cilantro)

The Rabbit Food Pyramid

A healthy diet for a domestic rabbit looks like this:

🌾
80% — Unlimited Timothy Hay or Orchard Grass

The foundation of every rabbit's diet. Always available.

🥬
10% — Salad Greens

2–3 Romaine leaves, a sprig or two of parsley, twice daily.

💊
5% — Supplements

Digestive support wafers, laxatone.

🥕
5% — Healthy Treats

Apple branches, 1 baby carrot. Occasional!
