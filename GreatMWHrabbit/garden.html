Types of Rabbits

Garden Rabbits (Cottontails)

I live OUTSIDE!
  • Cottontails — a different species from domestic rabbits
    • Cannot breed with domestics
    • Different immunities
    • Lifespan <3 years
  • 30-day gestation period
  • Doe (female) has two uteri — may produce 2 litters (of 2–12 babies) within the 30-day period
  • Babies weaned in 2 weeks
